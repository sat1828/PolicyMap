## [1.0.0] — May 2025

### Initial Release

**Report (PolicyMap_Report_v1.0.docx)**
- Full structured report: Executive Summary, Methodology, 4 Platform Profiles, Cross-Platform Matrix, 23-gap Gap Analysis, 4 Recommendations, Appendices A/B/C
- ~10,000 words, ~35 pages
- All policy claims sourced from primary platform policy documents accessed May 2025

**Audit Matrix (PolicyMap_Audit_Matrix.xlsx)**
- Tab 1: Master Matrix — 48-cell specificity score table (12 categories × 4 platforms) with enforcement, appeal, exception Y/N, source URLs, verbatim quotes
- Tab 2: Gap Register — all 23 gaps with Platform A/B quotes, real-world scenarios, severity ratings, harmonisation recommendations
- Tab 3: Platform Profiles — aggregate scores, unique features, strength ratings, one-sentence assessments

**Decision Tree (PolicyMap_ViolenceDecisionTree.html)**
- 6-node YES/NO flowchart for evaluating real-world violent content across all four platforms
- Colour-coded: Red (remove), Amber (restrict), Grey (human review), Blue (platform-specific), Green (document and close)
- Printable to PDF via browser
- Includes TikTok FYF gap callout (GAP-003)

**Research Notes**
- YouTube_notes.md: 9 verbatim policy quotes, EDSA framework documentation, specificity score rationale
- Reddit_notes.md: 10 verbatim quotes, misinformation absence documentation, NY S895B filing corroboration
- TikTok_notes.md: 15 verbatim quotes, FYF tier documentation, regional modulation, off-platform conduct review
- Meta_notes.md: 8 verbatim quotes, January 2025 Hateful Conduct change documentation, Oversight Board, Newsworthiness Allowance data

**Sources**
- sources_master_list.csv: 34 primary source URLs with platform, category, access date, and report section mapping
- policy_excerpts.md: Curated verbatim quotes organised by platform and category
